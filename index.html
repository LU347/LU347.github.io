About Me

Since childhood, I’ve always been drawn to technology—whether it was playing Super Mario on my Game Boy, tinkering with a red floppy disk, or exploring flash games. Moving to the U.S., my curiosity grew when my parents gifted me a Linux laptop, sparking my love for computers. In high school, I learned about electronics and even designed a microchip. Although I explored many career paths at UTSA, I’ve now found my focus in Full-Stack development. I may have taken some time to get here, but I’m excited for what’s ahead.
